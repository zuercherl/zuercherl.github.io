Intro

Hello

I am adding Java and other web development languages and experience to my many years of IT and Engineering Project Management experience in both pure software and integrated electronics and avionics experience base.

I have years of experience to bring to your company's skill base in both traditional SDLC and Agile environments. Much of my experience as a project manager has been a a fireman or bullpen pitcher, taking over as lead project manager of at risk projects in terms of either or both cost and schedule. I have experience in teambuilding, project course correction and working with stakeholders to get projects back on track and customers deliverables delivered as committed.

While I am used to running large integrated teams involving many different disciplines as my leads, I also have experience as a project manager in smaller team SCRUM and Agile environments.

Enjoy perusing through my portfolio and I hope that something pricks your interest to imagine how my skills and experience can be put to work for you and your company as you strive to accomplish your goals.

Sincerely, Leon Zuercher
